Sparsh Super 16

High Endurance

Enhanced ply density and vibration-dampening for demanding hospitality builds. Built to withstand heavy use while maintaining acoustic integrity.

Key Features

Reinforced Core

16mm equivalent density with optimized veneer lamination for superior screw-holding capacity.

Acoustic Performance

Specialized core construction dampens vibrations and provides excellent sound insulation properties.

Heavy-Duty Press Cycle

Extended pressing and curing process creates exceptionally stable boards resistant to movement.

Load Capacity

Enhanced bearing strength ideal for shelving, structural elements, and high-traffic applications.

  • Theater and auditorium installations
  • Recording studios and music venues
