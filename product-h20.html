Sparsh H2O

Water Resilient

Marine-inspired resilience for kitchens, baths, and moisture-prone environments. Stands firm against humidity and splashes.

Key Features

Boiling Water Proof

Withstands boiling water and extreme moisture conditions without delamination or swelling.

E1 Certified

Meets international low-emission standards (E1) for safe use in residential environments.

4-Press Technology

Advanced 4-stage pressing process creates superior bonding and enhanced moisture resistance.

Marine-Grade Adhesive

Marine-grade bonding resins prevent delamination and moisture penetration in humid conditions.
