Sparsh Shuttering

Formwork

Durable formwork panels engineered to endure repetitive concrete pours with clean release and consistent surface finish.

Key Features

High Reuse Cycles

Engineered for 50+ concrete pours with minimal wear, reducing project costs through repeated use.

Moisture-Shielded Faces

Specialized phenolic coating on contact surfaces ensures easy concrete release and extended panel life.

Stable Under Load

Reinforced core structure handles heavy concrete loads without sagging or deflection during curing.

Clean Surface Finish

Produces smooth concrete finish reducing on-site grinding and finishing labor requirements.

Ideal Applications

  • Concrete floor and deck formwork
  • Wall shutter systems for multi-story projects
  • Column and beam formwork
  • Staircase and ramp molds
  • Bridge deck construction
  • Industrial concrete structures
  • High-rise residential and commercial projects
  • Basement and foundation work
